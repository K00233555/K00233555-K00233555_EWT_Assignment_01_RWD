So who are we?

We're a community of like minded music fans who want to see a change in how reviews are done. We want the TRUE opinion of what makes a good album, not hyperbole! Are the riffs stompin', is there a groove, is the bass in your face?

How do we operate?

Good question. We operate as a community and as such we need frank and honest reviews. However we do have some rules, such as ...

  • No bad language!
  • Proper structured English please.
  • Reviews not to exceed 500 words

Nothing too restrictive as you can see.

So how do I become a reviewer?

We act on a policy of peer controlled reviews i.e. on registering as a member, you will work your way up through the ranks. Through discussions on the Forums board (almost ready folks!!!), you will build up a reputation and after a minimum of 20 posts, you will become a Junior Reviewer. This entitles you to submit one review per month on an album that has

  1. Been released for over 10 years.
  2. Has already been reviewed on the site

You don't need to agree with the old review, but you MUST make a good argument for your case.

Promotion and rewards ...

Well, after a while, your reputation will grow. How?There will be functionality to assess both your views and style of writing - while we don't want all out bedlam, we don't want conformity of opinion either. We want the true questions to be pondered upon, debated and drawn out, such as ...

  • Would St. Anger have been released had Cliff been around?
  • Orbital or the Orb?
  • Is Chesney Hawkes the one and only?

Through mod supervision and healthy debate, we will be promoting people to Reviewers and Head Reviewers. As for the rewards, through our sponsors, we will have items such as

  • Gig Tickets!
  • Albums!
  • Bandcamp subscriptions and tokens!
  • Meet and Greet access!

So what are you waiting for? Sign in right now!
